We are building a power strip that can be wirelessly controlled. There exist energy saving devices on the market (appliances, bulbs, laptops etc). But what if you do not want to swap out everything in your home and at the same time realize that your appliances are indeed wasting energy. You can simple hook up your devices to our power strip. These power strips are coded and controllable wirelessly. Through our app you can see which of your devices are turned on and turn them off with the click of a button.
